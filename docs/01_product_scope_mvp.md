This document defines what is INCLUDED and EXCLUDED from the MVP.

---

## MVP Includes
- Family creation
- Parent authentication
- Hero creation (kids + parents)
- Individual tasks
- Group quests
- XP system
- Levels
- Streaks
- Badges (basic challenge-based)
- Home HQ Dashboard
- Task and quest completion without approval

---

## Explicitly NOT in MVP
- Task approval flows
- Chat or messaging
- External rewards marketplace
- Social sharing
- Public profiles
- Ads
- AI-generated tasks

---

## Nice-to-Have (Post-MVP)
- Notifications (push)
- Seasonal events
- Advanced analytics
- Avatar customization packs
- Smart task suggestions

---

## Copilot Scope Rule
When generating features or code, assume MVP scope unless explicitly stated otherwise.
Do not introduce approval flows or social features.
